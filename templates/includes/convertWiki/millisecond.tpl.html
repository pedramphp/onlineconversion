Millisecond

Read more about Millisecond

A millisecond (from milli- and second; abbreviation: ms) is a thousandth (1/1,000) of a second. Its symbol is ms. One millisecond is to one second as one second is to 16.67 minutes.

10 milliseconds (a hundredth of a second) are called a centisecond, commonly seen on many stopwatches but often mistaken for milliseconds.

100 milliseconds (one tenth of a second) are called a decisecond.
